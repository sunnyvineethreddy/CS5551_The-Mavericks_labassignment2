Smart Health

Motivation

To provide information about diseases in particular area and also provide basic medicine, show them the availability of medicine in the nearest pharmacy

Description

Our application takes data from social networking sites based on the Posts of the users and find the locations where people are suffering from diseases and also give alert to the users present in that particular location. If the users are suffering from any disease our application will suggest prescribed medicines for that disease. It will show the locations of pharmacy where the medicines available.
Tasks In our Project are:
1. Collect Data from Social Media
2. Mark the locations where people are suffering from diseases
3. Give alerts to the people in that location to take precautions
4. Display the location of the pharmacy where medicine for that disease is available
